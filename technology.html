Technology

The physical
model

A scale model of a real home, built from scratch to demonstrate Domus Control working with actual hardware — in real time.

Devices

What's inside

Every feature in the app has a real counterpart built into the model. Control them remotely and watch them respond almost instantly.

Lights

LED strips installed per room. Toggle or dim them remotely, set schedules or let sleep mode turn them off automatically.

Shutters

Step motors open and close the window shutters. Fully controllable with timers and daily schedules from the app.

Door

A servo motor locks and unlocks the front door. Every unlock sends a push notification to all family members.

Heater

A Peltier cell simulates heating and cooling. Adjusts automatically based on live outdoor temperature from OpenWeather.

Speakers

Integrated with Spotify. Control music playback directly from the app without switching between apps.

Sensors

Temperature sensors feed live readings back to Firebase. Used by the app to make automatic heating decisions.

Hardware

Components used

Every component was chosen to keep the system responsive, reliable and close to what a real smart home would use.

ESP32 Microcontroller

The brain of the model. Connects to Wi-Fi, listens to Firebase and controls every device in real time.

Step Motors

Drive the shutter mechanism. Precise angle control allows full open, full close and everything in between.

Servo Motor

Controls the door lock. Rotates to a locked or unlocked position on command from the app.

Peltier Cell

Thermoelectric module that heats or cools depending on polarity. Used to simulate a real home heater.

LED Strips

Installed across all rooms of the model. Individually addressable and controlled per room via Firebase.

Temperature Sensor

Reads the ambient temperature inside the model and reports back to the app in real time.
