Context

The project in context

Domus Control was born as part of the PAP — Prova de Aptidão Profissional — of the IT Systems Management and Programming course (GPSI), at Escola Profissional Bento de Jesus Caraça, in the 2025/2026 academic year.

The goal was to demonstrate the technical skills acquired over three years, integrating software development, electronics and design into a real, functional solution.

Motivation

Why this project?

Most people have no real idea how much energy their home uses, or where the waste comes from. Appliances left on by accident, no remote control, no automation — these are everyday problems.

Domus Control came from the desire to fix that with accessible technology — combining an intuitive app, real-time communication and a physical scale model that makes the system tangible and demonstrable.
