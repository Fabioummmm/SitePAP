About the App

What is
Domus Control?

A mobile application that lets you monitor and control your home's energy consumption — remotely, in real time, from anywhere.

Overview

Smart home, simplified

Most people have no clear picture of how much energy their home uses, or where it's being wasted. Domus Control gives you a live dashboard of every connected device, lets you switch them on or off remotely, and helps you build smarter habits through automation and scheduling.

Features

What it does

Everything you need to manage your home from one place.

Remote Control

Switch lights, shutters, doors and heaters on or off from anywhere. Changes reflect on the hardware in under 2 seconds.

Energy Dashboard

View daily and hourly consumption per device. Spot patterns and understand where your electricity goes.

Automation

Set timers, daily schedules and sleep mode. The heater adjusts automatically based on real outdoor temperature.

Family Sharing

Share control with family members via a 6-digit code or direct link, with admin-managed roles.

Notifications

Get alerts when a device changes state, a timer ends, or a family event occurs — even in the background.

Spotify

Connect your smart speakers directly to your Spotify account and control playback from the app.
